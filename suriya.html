SURIYA

Suriya, born Saravanan Sivakumar on July 23, 1975, is a renowned Indian actor and film producer primarily known for his work in Tamil cinema. He is the son of actor Sivakumar and has a younger brother, Karthi, who is also an actor. Suriya married actress Jyothika in 2006, and they have two children.
